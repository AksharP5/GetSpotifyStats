    Check out the GitHub repo for this project

    Spotify Stats is a place where you can find all the information you have been searching for related to your spotify account. Are you tired of listening to the same music? Generate some recommended songs based on some of the artists, songs, and genres that you enjoy. Do you want to know what the most recent songs you listened to were? Are you curious about your top artists or songs? You can access all of this information here, with just a few clicks.

    The login process is fully secure, as you can find this service under your allowed apps in your Spotify account, and remove access if needed. The website was created by AP, please reach out with any feedback!
